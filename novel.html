Before you read

Before you start to read you should learn that NEVEREND are all written by Chinese (CH-zh), in case you don't know this language.

DAYDREAM( part 1 of NEVEREND)

Mainly written when I was in high school，it is just full of childhood imagination，a fresh beginning of the whole story.

TWILIGHT( part 2 of NEVEREND)

Slowly done when I was in college，with muffled anxiety and blindness for future，some parts and issues are not well completed。

FOREVER( part 3 of NEVEREND)

Basically written in the end of college time，just some desperated episodes, I was entering into social life and those vivid characters somehow became far to me 。
